Topic: The Solar System
- The solar system consists of the Sun, eight planets, and other celestial objects.
- The eight planets are Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune.
- The four inner planets (Mercury, Venus, Earth, and Mars) are called terrestrial planets.
- The four outer planets (Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune) are called gas giants.
- Pluto was once considered the ninth planet but was reclassified as a dwarf planet in 2006.
